BMI Categories

Underweight

BMI: Below 18.5

May indicate malnutrition or health issues

Normal Weight

BMI: 18.5 - 24.9

Healthy weight range for most adults

BMI: 25.0 - 29.9

Simple vs Compound Interest

Simple Interest

  • Interest calculated only on principal
  • Interest amount remains constant
  • Linear growth over time
  • Easier to calculate

Compound Interest

  • Interest calculated on principal + accumulated interest
  • Interest amount increases over time
  • Exponential growth over time
  • More complex calculation
