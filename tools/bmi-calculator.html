BMI Calculator

Calculate your Body Mass Index and get health recommendations

BMI Categories

Underweight

BMI: Below 18.5

May indicate malnutrition or health issues

Normal Weight

BMI: 18.5 - 24.9

Healthy weight range for most adults

Overweight

BMI: 25.0 - 29.9

May increase risk of health problems

Obese

BMI: 30.0 and above

Significantly increased health risks

Important Notes

  • BMI is a screening tool and not a diagnostic measure
  • It doesn't account for muscle mass, bone density, or body composition
  • Athletes may have high BMI due to muscle mass, not fat
  • Consult healthcare professionals for personalized health advice
  • BMI ranges may vary for different ethnic groups
